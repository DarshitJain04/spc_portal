  B.Tech

The Institute offers the following Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) Programs:
1. B.Tech. (Computer Science & Engineering)
2. B.Tech. (Electrical Engineering)
3. B.Tech. (Mechanical Engineering)

During 2010-14, the Institute had admitted students to the following B.Tech. Programs:
1. B.Tech. (System Science)
2. B.Tech. (Biologically Inspired System Science)
Admissions to these programs are stopped from July 2015

1. M.Tech. (Information & Communication Technology)
